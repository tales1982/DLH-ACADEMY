================================================================================
          CIS CONTROLS v8 -- SUMMARY REFERENCE
          Implementation Groups: IG1 (Essential), IG2 (Foundational)
          Compiled for Project 0x03: The Defense Blueprint
================================================================================

STRUCTURE:
  18 Controls, each containing multiple Safeguards
  IG1: 56 safeguards (minimum baseline for ALL organizations)
  IG2: 74 additional safeguards (for organizations with moderate risk)
  IG3: 23 additional safeguards (for organizations with advanced threats)

  MedDefense Target: IG1 fully implemented, IG2 partially implemented

========================================================================
CONTROL 1: Inventory and Control of Enterprise Assets
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    1.1 Establish and maintain detailed enterprise asset inventory
    1.2 Address unauthorized assets (remove, quarantine, or document)
  Key Question: Do you know every device on your network?

CONTROL 2: Inventory and Control of Software Assets
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    2.1 Establish and maintain software inventory
    2.2 Ensure authorized software is currently supported
    2.3 Address unauthorized software
  Key Question: Do you know every application running in your environment?

CONTROL 3: Data Protection
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    3.1 Establish and maintain a data management process
    3.2 Establish and maintain a data inventory
    3.3 Configure data access control lists
    3.4 Enforce data retention
    3.6 Encrypt data on end-user devices
  Key Question: Is sensitive data classified, controlled, and encrypted?

CONTROL 4: Secure Configuration of Enterprise Assets and Software
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    4.1 Establish and maintain secure configuration process
    4.2 Establish and maintain secure configuration for network infra
    4.3 Configure automatic session locking on enterprise assets
    4.4 Implement and manage a firewall on servers
    4.5 Implement and manage a firewall on end-user devices
    4.6 Securely manage enterprise assets and software
    4.7 Manage default accounts on enterprise assets and software
  Key Question: Are systems hardened beyond default configurations?

CONTROL 5: Account Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    5.1 Establish and maintain an inventory of accounts
    5.2 Use unique passwords
    5.3 Disable dormant accounts (after 45 days)
    5.4 Restrict administrator privileges to dedicated accounts
  Key Question: Are all accounts inventoried, unique, and monitored?

CONTROL 6: Access Control Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    6.1 Establish an access granting process
    6.2 Establish an access revoking process
    6.3 Require MFA for externally-exposed applications
    6.4 Require MFA for remote network access
    6.5 Require MFA for administrative access
  Key Question: Is access controlled, revoked timely, and MFA-enforced?

CONTROL 7: Continuous Vulnerability Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    7.1 Establish and maintain a vulnerability management process
    7.2 Establish and maintain a remediation process
    7.3 Perform automated OS patch management
    7.4 Perform automated application patch management
  Key Question: Are vulnerabilities identified, prioritized, and patched?

CONTROL 8: Audit Log Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    8.1 Establish and maintain an audit log management process
    8.2 Collect audit logs
    8.3 Ensure adequate audit log storage
  Key Question: Are security-relevant events logged and retained?

CONTROL 9: Email and Web Browser Protections
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    9.1 Ensure use of only fully supported browsers and email clients
    9.2 Use DNS filtering services
  Key Question: Are email and browser attack vectors mitigated?

CONTROL 10: Malware Defenses
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    10.1 Deploy and maintain anti-malware software
    10.2 Configure automatic anti-malware signature updates
    10.3 Disable autorun and autoplay for removable media
  Key Question: Are endpoints protected against malware?

CONTROL 11: Data Recovery
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    11.1 Establish and maintain a data recovery process
    11.2 Perform automated backups
    11.3 Protect recovery data (isolate or encrypt)
    11.4 Establish and maintain an isolated instance of recovery data
  Key Question: Can you recover from data loss? Are backups isolated?

CONTROL 12: Network Infrastructure Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    12.1 Ensure network infrastructure is up to date
  IG2 Safeguards:
    12.2 Establish and maintain a secure network architecture
    12.3 Securely manage network infrastructure
    12.4 Establish and maintain architecture diagrams
    12.5 Centralize network authentication, authorization, enrollment
    12.6 Use secure network management and communication protocols
    12.7 Ensure remote devices use a VPN and connect to managed DNS
    12.8 Establish and maintain dedicated computing resources for admin
  Key Question: Is the network architecture secure and segmented?

CONTROL 13: Network Monitoring and Defense
========================================================================
  IG2 Safeguards:
    13.1 Centralize security event alerting
    13.3 Deploy a network intrusion detection solution
    13.6 Collect network traffic flow logs
    13.8 Deploy a network intrusion prevention solution
  Key Question: Are network anomalies and intrusions detected?

CONTROL 14: Security Awareness and Skills Training
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    14.1 Establish and maintain a security awareness program
    14.2 Train workforce to recognize social engineering attacks
    14.3 Train workforce on authentication best practices
    14.4 Train workforce on data handling best practices
    14.5 Train workforce on causes of unintentional data exposure
    14.6 Train workforce on recognizing and reporting security incidents
  Key Question: Are all users trained to recognize and respond to threats?

CONTROL 15: Service Provider Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    15.1 Establish and maintain an inventory of service providers
    15.2 Establish and maintain a service provider management policy
  Key Question: Are third-party providers inventoried and managed?

CONTROL 16: Application Software Security
========================================================================
  IG2 Safeguards:
    16.1 Establish and maintain a secure application development process
    16.9 Train developers in application security
  Key Question: Is internally developed software secure?

CONTROL 17: Incident Response Management
========================================================================
  IG1 Safeguards:
    17.1 Designate personnel to manage incident handling
    17.2 Establish and maintain contact information for reporting
    17.3 Establish and maintain an enterprise process for reporting
  Key Question: Can the organization respond to a security incident?

CONTROL 18: Penetration Testing
========================================================================
  IG2 Safeguards:
    18.1 Establish and maintain a penetration testing program
    18.2 Perform periodic external penetration tests
    18.3 Remediate penetration test findings
  Key Question: Is the organization testing its own defenses?
