# 16. The Threat Priority Assessment

**Goal:** Produce the definitive ranking of threats against MedDefense with actionable recommendations.

## Context

This is the verdict. Everything you have built — the actor profiles, the vectors, the surfaces, the kill chains, the STRIDE analysis, the scenarios, the gap correlation — distills into a single question: What are the 5 things most likely to hurt MedDefense, and what should be done about each one?

## Instructions

Produce a Prioritized Threat Assessment for the Top 5 threats to MedDefense. Rank them from most to least critical.

For each threat:

```
Rank: [1-5]
Threat: [One-line description]
Actor Type: [From T6]
Primary Vector: [Most likely entry method]
Primary Target: [MedDefense asset]
Likelihood: [Critical/High/Medium/Low - justify with sector data + MedDefense specifics]
Impact: [Critical/High/Medium/Low - justify with asset criticality from 1x00]
Overall Priority: [Composite assessment]
Key Gap: [The single gap from 1x00 whose closure would most reduce this threat]
Recommended Action: [One concrete, specific action with effort estimate: Quick Win / Short-term / Long-term]
```

After the Top 5, write a **Strategic Recommendation** (one paragraph): If MedDefense could only fund 2 defensive initiatives in the next quarter based on your threat analysis, what should they be and why?
