# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes acceptable use requirements for company systems, networks, devices, and data.

The objective is to protect company information, reduce security risks, and ensure responsible use of technology resources.

# 2. Scope

## 2.1 Applicability

This policy applies to:

* All employees
* Contractors and consultants
* Third-party vendors with access to company systems
* Remote workers

## 2.2 Systems/Assets Covered

* Corporate email
* VPN services
* Cloud development environment
* Collaboration tools
* Company devices
* Corporate network

## 2.3 Exclusions

* Personal systems not connected to company resources

# 3. Policy Statements

## 3.1 Internet Usage

Company internet access must be used primarily for business purposes.

Requirements:

* Access only authorized websites.
* Limited personal browsing is allowed during breaks.
* Access to illegal, offensive, or malicious websites is prohibited.
* Internet activity may be monitored by the company.

## 3.2 Email Usage

Corporate email must be used professionally.

Requirements:

* Do not use company email for personal business.
* Do not send confidential information without authorization.
* Suspicious emails must be reported immediately.
* Email records must be retained according to company requirements.

## 3.3 Software Usage

Only approved software may be used.

Requirements:

* Unauthorized software installation is prohibited.
* Software requests must be approved by IT.
* Software must come from trusted sources.
* Security updates must not be disabled.

## 3.4 Social Media Usage

Employees must protect company information.

Requirements:

* Do not share confidential projects online.
* Do not disclose customer information.
* Employees may not speak on behalf of the company without authorization.
* Company reputation must be protected at all times.

## 3.5 BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

Personal devices must meet security requirements.

Requirements:

* Devices must be registered with IT.
* MFA must be enabled.
* Devices must use screen lock protection.
* Devices must run current security updates.

## 3.6 Data Handling

Company data must be protected throughout its lifecycle.

Requirements:

* Store data only in approved locations.
* Share data only with authorized users.
* Sensitive data must be encrypted.
* Data must be securely deleted when no longer required.

| -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Executive Management | Approve policy and support compliance                          |
| IT Security Team     | Implement controls, monitor compliance, investigate violations |
| Department Managers  | Ensure team compliance and report issues                       |
| IT Department        | Approve software and manage devices                            |
| All Employees        | Follow policy requirements and report incidents                |

# 5. Compliance

## 5.1 Monitoring

The company may monitor internet usage, email activity, software installations, and device compliance.

## 5.2 Reporting

Employees must report security incidents and policy violations immediately.

## 5.3 Auditing

Compliance audits will be performed annually or when required.

# 6. Enforcement

## 6.1 Violations

Violations of this policy may result in:

* Verbal warning
* Written warning
* Suspension of access privileges
* Disciplinary action
* Termination of employment
* Legal action where applicable

## 6.2 Reporting Violations

Suspected violations must be reported to:

[security@techsecure.com](mailto:security@techsecure.com)

# 7. Exceptions

## 7.1 Exception Process

Exceptions require:

1. Written request
2. Business justification
3. Risk assessment
4. Management approval
5. Documented exception record

## 7.2 Exception Duration

All exceptions must have an expiration date and be reviewed annually.
